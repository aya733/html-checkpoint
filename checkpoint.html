about

Friends is a beloved American television sitcom that aired from 1994 to 2004, created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman.
Set in New York City, the show revolves around a group of six friends as they navigate the ups and downs of life,
love, and careers in their twenties and thirties.

work

Iconic Settings: Central Perk, the friends' coffeehouse hangout, became a cultural icon synonymous with the show's sense of community and comfort.

Global Appeal: The universal themes of friendship, love, and personal growth resonate with audiences worldwide,
making "Friends" a timeless favorite across cultures and generations.
